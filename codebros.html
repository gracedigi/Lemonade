About Us!


We are local lemon farmers from Tennessee with big lemons but even bigger dreams! We first became interested in lemonade when we were little kids. We always had a passion for lemonade stands and have carried this with us throughout our lives.

Come visit us at our many locations worldwide and come grab a glass of liquid gold!
