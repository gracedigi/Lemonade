When Life Gives You Lemons. . .

The Process


There are few things more satisfying than a cool, refreshing glass of lemonade on a hot summer day! We're here to show you how to make the best lemonade your taste buds will ever encounter.


Ingredients

  • Lemons

  • Sugar

  • Water

Step 1:

Combine 1 3/4 cups of sugar and one cup of water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil.


Step 2:

Let the sugar dissolve and then allow this syrup to chill in the refrigerator.


Step 3:

Squeeze your farm fresh lemons into a clean bowl.



Step 4:

Strain the lemon juice to collect unwanted seeds and pulp.



Step 5:

Mix together syrup and lemon juice, and add 7 more cups of water.




Pour yourself a refreshing glass of farm fresh lemonade! Enjoy!
